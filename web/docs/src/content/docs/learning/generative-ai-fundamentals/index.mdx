This course is based on Lab34's enterprise AI training programme. In five focused lessons you will build a solid understanding of how generative AI actually works — no hype, just mechanics. By the end you will know what tokens are, how predictions happen, which model families exist, and why context windows matter more than most people think.

## What you will learn

- What AI and generative AI actually are (and what they are not)
- How tokenisation works and why it matters
- The next-token prediction mechanic at the core of every large language model
- Major model families and their trade-offs
- Knowledge cut-off dates, context, and context-window management

## Prerequisites

- No programming experience required
- Curiosity about how AI tools work under the hood
